高斯分布中的kl散度

p服从N($\mu_1$,$\sigma_1^2$)，q服从N($\mu_2$,$\sigma_2^2$)
$$
KL(p,q)=
\int_xe^{-\frac { {(x-\mu_1)}^2}{2\sigma_1^2} }log\frac{\frac 1{\sqrt{2\pi}\sigma_1}e^{-\frac{(x-\mu_1)^2}{2\sigma_1^2} } }{\frac 1{\sqrt{2\pi}\sigma_2}e^{-\frac{(x-\mu_2)^2}{2\sigma_2^2} } }dx=
\int_xe^{-\frac { {(x-\mu_1)}^2}{2\sigma_1^2} }[log\frac {\sigma_2}{\sigma_1}-\frac{(x-\mu_1)^2}{2\sigma_1^2}+\frac {(x-\mu2)^2}{2\sigma_2^2}]dx
$$
第一项
$$
log\frac{\sigma_2}{\sigma_1}\int_x\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}\sigma_1}e^{-\frac{(x-\mu_1)^2}{2\sigma_1^2} }=log\frac{\sigma_2}{\sigma_1}
$$
第二项
$$
-\frac{1}{2\sigma_1^2}\int_x(x-\mu_1)^2\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}\sigma_1}e^{-\frac{(x-\mu_1)^2}{2\sigma_1^2} }dx=-\frac{1}{2\sigma_1^2}\sigma_1^2=-\frac{1}{2}
$$
第三项
$$
\frac{1}{2\sigma_2^2}\int_x(x-\mu_2)^2\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}\sigma_1}e^{-\frac{(x-\mu1)^2}{2\sigma_1^2} }dx
=\frac{1}{2\sigma_2^2}\int_x(x^2-2\mu_2x+\mu_2^2)\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}\sigma_1}e^{-\frac{(x-\mu_1)^2}{2\sigma_1^2} }dx\
=\frac{\sigma_1^2+\mu_1^2-2\mu_1\mu_2+\mu_2^2}{2\sigma_2^2}=\frac{\sigma_1^2+(\mu_1-\mu_2)^2}{2\sigma_2^2}
$$
所以最终可以求得p和q之间的KL散度
$$
KL(p,q)=log\frac{\sigma_2}{\sigma_1}+\frac{\sigma_1^2+(\mu_1-\mu_2)}{2\sigma_2^2}-\frac12
$$
